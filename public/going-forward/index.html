Is this strategy any good?

We’ve read a lot of strategy at this point in the book. We can judge a strategy’s format, and its construction: both are useful things. However, format is a predictor of quality, not quality itself. The remaining question is, how should we assess whether a strategy is any good? Uber’s service migration strategy unlocked the entire organization to make rapid progress. It also led to a sprawling architecture problem down the line.
